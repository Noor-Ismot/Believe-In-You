Which Motivate Me?

What motivates people most? Because just as people have different personalities, they also have different core motivators (McClelland, 1987). Three core needs drive our behaviour and thus influence what motivates us: Achievement (getting things done), Power (having influence over others) and Belonging (having good relationships).
